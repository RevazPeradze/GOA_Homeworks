About Georgian Cuisine

Georgian cuisine is a blend of rich flavors and diverse influences, reflecting the country's position at the crossroads of East and West. Known for its hearty meals, aromatic spices, and traditional cooking methods, Georgian food is a must-try for any culinary enthusiast.

Famous Dishes

Khinkali

Juicy dumplings filled with spiced meat or vegetables, traditionally eaten by hand to savor the flavorful broth inside.

Khachapuri

A cheese-filled bread that comes in various regional styles. The Adjarian version is boat-shaped with an egg yolk on top.

Mtsvadi

Grilled meat skewers cooked over an open flame, often enjoyed with fresh herbs and homemade wine.

Churchkhela

A traditional Georgian snack made by dipping strings of nuts into grape must and drying them into a chewy candy.

Lobio

A hearty bean stew seasoned with coriander, onions, and sometimes walnuts, served with cornbread or pickles.
